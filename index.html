Game & Media Audio · Interactive & Linear Sound

Whether you need sound for games, trailers, interactive experiences or podcasts, I can handle everything from original sound design and music to technical implementation.

Game sound design
creates responsive SFX, UI and ambiences that fit your mechanics and pacing.

Adaptive music
turns your soundtrack into a living score that reacts to gameplay and states.

Wwise implementation
hooks your audio into the engine with events, RTPCs, states and switches.

Trailers & linear media
create music, sound design and editing for impactful promos and videos.

Audio cleanup & restoration
uses modern tools to reduce noise and artefacts, turning rough audio and dialogue recordings into something usable and intelligible.
